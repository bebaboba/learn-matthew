About

The short version

I'm an L&D producer with roots in product management and arts & culture. That unusual combination means I approach learning experiences the way a PM approaches a product: with user empathy, a bias for iteration, and a deep respect for what makes something genuinely engaging.

I've built curriculum for fast-moving teams, managed cross-functional launches, and produced cultural programming — often at the same time. I'm at my best when the problem is ambiguous and the stakes are real.
